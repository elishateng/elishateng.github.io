1. Are you eligible to legally work in the country where the position to which you are applying is located? 
Answer: No, I'll require sponsorship for an employment permit to work in Ireland.

2. Please clarify your work authorization for the Country you are applying to ?
Answer: I do not currently hold an employment permit for Ireland. I understand the requirement for a Critical Skills Employment Permit and will require the company's sponorship to obtain it.

3. Please indicate your desired target compensation for this role.
Answer: 55000-7000 per annum, depending on the comprehensive benefits package

4. When are you available to start working/please clarity your notice period if any?
Answer: Available 3-4 months after the job offer, allowing time for the visa/permit application and relocation process.

5. Will you now or in the future require sponorship?
Answer: Yes, I currently require sponorship for an initial work permit. My goal is long-term employment with the company.
